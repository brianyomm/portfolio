company overview: Next Retail was founded in 2017 with $5M in seed funding to design the future of e-commerce. By utilizing XR (extended reality), we created branded commerce experiences aimed at enhancing the retail shopping experience. Our ultimate goal was to enable customers to interact with brands in a new and engaging way.

nextretail

my role

As the UI/UX design Lead, I led the creation of immersive 3D e-commerce stores for prominent brands such as Ethan Allen, Restoration Hardware, and Oscar de la Renta. Our goal was to replicate the in-store shopping experience online, allowing customers to explore flagship store offerings through detailed 3D models.

focusing primarily on product features, I designed the entire ui/ux stack for all branded experiences and worked alongside 5 in-house engineers and 5 oversea engineers. We delivered branded shopping experiences for restoration hardware, ethan allen, oscar de la renta, fred segal, and more.
